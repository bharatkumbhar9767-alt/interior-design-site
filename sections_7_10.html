Our Story.

Built from experience.
Driven by belief.
The Spatial Edit was founded by Preksha Bhargav and Krishna Bhargav — partners in life, who started this studio for a very personal reason.

It didn't begin in a design studio. It began in a home that wasn't working, a home that didn't feel like theirs.

When they were expecting their first baby, in the third trimester, the couple moved into a new apartment. They had hired an interior designer.

What followed was one of the most chaotic experiences of their lives — delays, decisions that made no sense. They were experiencing their worst nightmare.
